Faq's

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you based?

I'm based in Novi Sad, Serbia, but I travel all over Europe to capture love.

How many photo will you receive?

You'll receive over 600-900 photos, depending on how many hours we shoot and how jam-packed your wedding day is!

How will you receive photos?

Firstly, you'll receive a link to your online gallery and slide slow to watch and relive your wedding day. A week or two after you'll also receive a beautiful package in the mail with a USB, selected prints and a little thank you note from me.

How long will it tako to receive your photos?

It will take roughly 4 to 6 weeks to receive your final wedding photos. I put a lot of love and care into perfecting each image, which is why it'll take me a little bit more time to deliver your final images. If you'd like your photos sooner, please ask me about the rush fee.

Will you get the full size images and edited?

You'll get fully processed high-resolution digital files. All photos are color-corrected and edited.

Do you have a payment plan?

Yes, we can organise to split your payments into two installments. Just let me know if this would suit you.
